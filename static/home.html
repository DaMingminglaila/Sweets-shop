Blueberry cake Sweet and juicy

A delicious dessert with blueberry flavor. Ingredients: 2-3 pieces of flying cake, appropriate ingredients of blueberry sauce: egg liquid, cooking oil practice: 1. Prepare blueberry sauce and flying cake skin. 2. Take one of the flying cakes and cut them in half. 3. Add some blueberry sauce to the center of the pie. 4. Cover the other half of the pie. 5. Press the fork along the outer edge of the crust to make the two layers sticky
